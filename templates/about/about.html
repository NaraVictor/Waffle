About Waffle

Waffle is a social learning platform which seeks to provide a stage for students especially, WAEC candidates, to learn and teach anyone around the world. Verified authors can create, upload and sell curated video materials, provide books or form learning communities on a particular topic where virtual classes can take place. There is a social networking platform built into waffle backed by a virtual personal assistant. There is more to explore and the future looks exciting
