Understanding that man is dynamic and society thrive on diversity, the goal of Waffle, a social learning platform, is to amplify the richness of:
  • The industrial education model (current system)
  • The agricultural education model
  • African culture (the fabric of our societies).
Waffle provides the following core tools to this effect:
  • Social learning platform
  • Library of videos and books
  • Learning Communities
  • Fellows

Social Learning

The social learning platform is first of many tools to be implemented and enables any user to ask and/or answer a question, provide a short educational content or promote an incredible talent worthy of support and/or attention.

Library

The video and books library will enable authors, publishers and other content creators to prepare and upload verified books and curated video contents for different disciplines and fields of work. However, we place special interest on (not in any special order):
  • WAEC, Nursing and Teacher training contents
  • African culture
  • Youth and gender educational contents
  • Innovations
  • Talents
  • Multi-disciplinary educational contents
These contents could be premium or freemium based on uploaderâ€™s discretion.

Communities

Learning communities are similar to Facebook pages in concept but optimized for teaching and learning. Users can follow or create a community and these communities could hold live sessions, create and post content local to the community for instance, there could be a community about the culture and history of Lawra (a town in upper west of Ghana) and much more.

Fellows

Fellows are growth partners of waffle. These could be individuals and/or bodies dedicated to giving back or supporting whatever interests them on the platform, Students who benefited from waffle and would like to act as guide for other students following similar paths, individuals or organizations interested in genuinely supporting others through the platform (for instance a promoted talent, brilliant but needy students etc), institutions/groups/bodies interested in localizing the ideology of waffle through workshops, meetups, conferences and such like initiatives. There is more on this side of the story coming up soon.

There are exciting adventures with waffle yet to be unraveled.
